About us

Welcome to Positive Vibes, the premier coach training agency located in Morocco! Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to achieve their fullest potential by providing world-class coaching training and development programs.

At Positive Vibes, we believe that coaching is not just a profession, but a way of life. Our team of experienced and certified coaches is dedicated to helping aspiring coaches develop their skills and build successful coaching practices. We offer a range of training programs that cater to different levels of experience and expertise, including introductory courses, advanced training, and specialized workshops

Our training programs are designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of coaching principles and techniques, as well as practical experience in working with clients. Our coaches use a holistic approach that takes into account the unique needs and goals of each client, helping them to develop personalized strategies for success.

We believe in creating a positive and supportive learning environment, where our students can grow and thrive. Our training programs are facilitated by experienced coaches who are committed to helping our students succeed. We also provide ongoing support and mentorship to our graduates, helping them to build thriving coaching practices and make a positive impact in their communities.

At Positive Vibes, we are dedicated to promoting the highest standards of professionalism and ethics in the coaching industry. Our training programs are accredited by the International Coach Federation (ICF), the leading professional association for coaches worldwide. We also adhere to the ICF Code of Ethics, which sets the highest standards of integrity and professionalism in coaching.

Whether you are an aspiring coach looking to launch your coaching practice or an experienced coach looking to take your skills to the next level, Positive Vibes has the training programs and support you need to achieve your goals. Contact us today to learn more about our training programs and start your journey towards becoming a successful coach!
